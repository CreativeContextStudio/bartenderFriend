## **Classic Cocktails — The Foundations (1-10)**

1. **Old Fashioned** — Whiskey, sugar, bitters, orange twist. The quintessential American classic that continues to dominate.

2. **Margarita** — Tequila, lime juice, triple sec, salt rim. The most searched and ordered cocktail across all regions.

3. **Espresso Martini** — Vodka, coffee liqueur, fresh espresso. The coffee cocktail taking bars by storm.

4. **Mojito** — White rum, mint, lime juice, sugar, soda. Perfect for warm Southeast evenings.

5. **Moscow Mule** — Vodka, ginger beer, lime wedge in a copper mug. Effervescent favorite.

6. **Negroni** — Gin, Campari, sweet vermouth. Bitter, balanced, and timeless.

7. **Martini** — Gin or vodka, dry vermouth. Available dirty, dry, or flavored.

8. **Manhattan** — Rye whiskey, sweet vermouth, bitters. Bold and sophisticated.

9. **Piña Colada** — Rum, coconut cream, pineapple juice. Tropical escape in a glass.

10. **Whiskey Sour** — Whiskey, lemon juice, simple syrup. Perfect balance of sweet and sour.

## **2026 Southeast Cocktail Trends**

The Southeast bar scene in 2026 is characterized by:

- **Classic cocktails remain king** — Old Fashioneds, Margaritas, and Martinis continue to dominate bar orders
- **Coffee-infused growth** — Espresso Martinis bridge the gap between daytime and nightlife
- **Low-ABV expansion** — Spritzes and aperitifs gain traction for social occasions
- **Ingredient innovation** — Mezcal, amaro, and regional spirits enhance traditional templates
- **Seasonal variations** — Herb-infused sours and botanical cocktails reflect local produce
