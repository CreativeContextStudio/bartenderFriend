# BARTENDER QUICK REFERENCE CHEAT SHEET

## SOUR TEMPLATE (Shake, Strain, Citrusy)
**2 oz spirit | ¾ oz lemon/lime | ¾ oz simple | Shake hard | Strain**

| Drink | Spec | Glass | Garnish |
|-------|------|-------|---------|
| **Margarita** | 2 oz tequila, 1 oz lime, ¾ oz triple sec, ¼ oz agave | Rocks or up | Lime wheel, salt rim |
| **Daiquiri** | 2 oz light rum, 1 oz lime, ¾ oz simple | Coupe (up) | Lime wheel |
| **Whiskey Sour** | 2 oz bourbon, ¾ oz lemon, ¾ oz simple | Rocks or up | Cherry + orange slice |
| **Cosmopolitan** | 1½ oz vodka, 1 oz Cointreau, ½ oz lime, ½ oz cranberry | Coupe (up) | Lime wheel |
| **Spicy Margarita** | Margarita + muddled jalapeño or chili syrup | Rocks | Lime wheel, salt rim |

## SPIRIT-FORWARD TEMPLATE (Stir, 20–30 sec, Boozy/Silky)
**Stir with ice | Strain into coupe or rocks | Peel or cherry**

| Drink | Spec | Glass | Garnish |
|-------|------|-------|---------|
| **Old Fashioned** | 2 oz bourbon/rye, ¼–½ oz simple, 2–3 dashes Angostura | Rocks, large ice | Orange peel |
| **Negroni** | 1 oz gin, 1 oz sweet vermouth, 1 oz Campari | Rocks, large ice | Orange peel |
| **Manhattan** | 2 oz rye/bourbon, 1 oz sweet vermouth, 2 dashes Angostura | Coupe (up) | Cherry |
| **Martini (Gin)** | 2½ oz gin, ½ oz dry vermouth | Coupe (up) | Lemon twist or olive |
| **Martini (Vodka)** | 2½ oz vodka, ½ oz dry vermouth | Coupe (up) | Lemon twist or olive |
| **Dirty Martini** | Martini + ½ oz olive brine (gin or vodka base) | Coupe (up) | Olives |

## KEY TERMS (Say these right)

- **Neat** = Spirit only, no ice
- **Rocks** = Over ice in rocks glass
- **Up** = Chilled & strained, NO ice in glass
- **Dry** = Less vermouth (martinis, manhattans)
- **Dirty** = Olive brine added (martinis)
- **Twist** = Citrus peel for garnish
- **Double** = Double the spirit (bigger glass, more ice)

---

## SHAKE vs STIR RULE

**SHAKE** → Anything with citrus, juice, egg white, dairy, syrup, or if drink is "sour-like"  
**STIR** → Clear only: spirits + liqueurs + vermouth (no juice/citrus)

---

## WORKFLOW TIPS

1. **Set up station**: Jigger, shaker, bar spoon, strainers, citrus, ice bucket, glassware within reach
2. **Batch drinks**: Build all "rocks" first, then all "up" shaken, then highballs (keeps temps right)
3. **Citrus prep**: Cut fresh lime/lemon wheels and wedges at start of shift
4. **Ice matters**: Use fresh ice; don't let shaker sit; ice melts = over-diluted drink
5. **Free pour**: Once confident, learn to eyeball 1.5 oz & 0.75 oz with a jigger as backup

## GUEST INTERACTION SCRIPTS

**"What would you recommend?"**  
→ "Do you like spirit-forward drinks, fruity & tart, or something smooth? Tequila, gin, or vodka person?"

**"Make me something not too sweet."**  
→ Old Fashioned, Negroni, Martini, Daiquiri

**"I like tequila and citrus, not too sweet."**  
→ Paloma, Margarita (light on agave), Daiquiri (with tequila instead of rum)

**"Something strong."**  
→ Old Fashioned, Negroni, Martini (spirit-forward builds)
